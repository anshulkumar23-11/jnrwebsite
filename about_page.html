About Page

The basic principal of of jnr is to place priority on quality in order to achieve true customer satisfaction. JNR is known for long term bussiness relationship, transparency, consistent quality competitiveness and social obligation towards the environment.

keeping the above motto in mind, a group of highly skilled and experienced proffesionals, in UV Coating & Curing applications, started JNR Enterprises, two years back in 2021. The skill base consists of top class designing, manufacturing, servicing, applications and sales marketing capablities. Since then, JNR Enterprises has installed more than 200 machines achieving, besides normal Glass & Matte effects, appreciative drip off effects and various types of spot coating effects like Bubble, Sand and glitter etc. with the support of the Industry, which was thriving for quality machines with warranted servicing and applications support, JNR Enterprises has won the laurels from 100% of its customers.
